4. Multi-Event Packages

Clients booking coverage across multiple events receive a bundled package rate. This may include any combination of events such as a reception, ceremony, or additional celebrations. Individual single-event pricing does not apply once a multi-event package has been agreed upon. Full package details and pricing are outlined in a separate addendum attached to this agreement.

5. Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy
Cancellation by Client:
  • More than 60 days before event: Retainer is forfeited; no additional fee.
  • 31–60 days before event: 50% of total contract value is due.
  • 30 days or fewer before event: 100% of total contract value is due.
Rescheduling by Client:

One (1) complimentary rescheduling allowed if the new date is available and notice is given at least 30 days in advance. Subsequent reschedules are subject to a $100 rescheduling fee.

Cancellation by MC:

In the unlikely event the MC must cancel, a full refund of all payments received will be issued and the MC will make reasonable efforts to provide a qualified referral.

6. Client Responsibilities
  • Provide a finalized run-of-show no later than 10 days before each event.
  • Ensure the MC has access to the venue at least 60 minutes before program start.
  • Provide meal service for the MC during the reception.
  • Provide a working microphone system (wired or wireless) through the venue or DJ.
  • Communicate any family-specific sensitivities, name pronunciations, or special requests in advance.
7. Media & Content Release

By signing this agreement, the Client grants the MC full permission to capture, use, and share behind-the-scenes content, highlight moments, and event footage for social media and promotional purposes. This release is a required condition of service and is non-negotiable.

8. Limitation of Liability

The MC is not responsible for delays or program changes caused by other vendors, venue staff, or circumstances outside his control. The MC's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid.

9. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Illinois. Any disputes shall be resolved in Cook or DuPage County, Illinois.

10. Entire Agreement

This document, along with any signed addenda, constitutes the entire agreement between both parties. Any modifications must be agreed upon in writing and signed by both parties.
